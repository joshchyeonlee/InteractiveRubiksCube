Controls:

Click and Drag mouse to rotate entire cube Hold shift to pan, scroll to zoom

To rotate faces, click on a segment and use the following keys:

Clockwise

Q: Rotate about the X-axis W: Rotate about the Y-axis E: Rotate about the Z-axis

Counter-clockwise

A: Rotate about the X-axis S: Rotate about the Y-axis D: Rotate about the Z-axis

Axes

Red: X-axis Green: Y-axis Blue: Z-axis
